About CJ

A passionate, curious tutor who focuses on building student enthusiasm for learning.

CJ's interest in tutoring stems from his passion for lifelong learning. Whether reading, exploring museums and historical sites, or taking classes, CJ strives to constantly learn - and thrives on sharing this love of learning with others. This passion led CJ into education - he taught Middle School Science for two years through Teach for America and currently works for Generation Code, a startup providing computer coding education to thousands of students.

Mentorship and role modeling are also important to CJ, who has coached and played soccer from the middle school to college levels and serves as a volunteer peer mentor. CJ has a track record of success - helping dozens of students drastically improve their standardized test scores and academic performance, and boosting their confidence in the process.

Qualifications

CJ scored in the 99th percentile on the ACT and GMAT, and in the 97th percentile on the SAT (score details available below) and knows what it takes to succeed on these tests. In addition, CJ has taken AP and college-level courses across a wide range of disciplines.

As an Economics major, CJ took significant quantitative coursework, particularly in Statistics (one of CJ's favorite subjects). CJ is also a published author - for research into infectious disease - and took Chemistry and upper-level Biology courses, serving as a TA for Cell Biology courses.

CJ loves languages and is fluent in Spanish after living in Mexico as a child. He is also proficient in web development and is comfortable with HTML/CSS, JavaScript, node.js, Express, React and SQL. Details on CJ's scores, education, and experience can be found below.
