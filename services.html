Standardized Tests

CJ's standardized test prep programs leverage data and your student's existing strengths to drive big results. Diagnostic tests help CJ build a plan for your student, and ongoing practice tests and homework sets provide data that ensures our sessions reflect the material that will boost scores. CJ identifies the sections that represent the highest opportunity for growth, even which may not be the section in which the student scores lowest.

CJ knows that every student has natural strengths. He works with your student to identify and leverage these strengths by applying them across sections. Doing so not only drives the biggest improvements, but also boosts learner confidence and engagement.

AP & Academic Subjects

Students learn best by doing. CJ's academic subject tutoring places a strong emphasis on getting students practice reps with the types of problems they will face in class or on the AP exam. In academic subject tutoring sessions, most of our time is spent working through practice problems. The result is a student confident in their abilities to solve real problems.

General Support

As a middle and high school student, CJ had trouble staying organized and developing successful study habits. These skills can be difficult to build - CJ is a firm believer in the power of academic mentoring to push students to do so. Mentoring is a passion for CJ, who has more than a decade of experience as a teacher, tutor, and soccer coach to students aged 7-18. CJ continues to sharpen his mentor abilities as a volunteer peer mentor, helping adults in fields like job searches and personal finances. General academic support focuses on helping students develop a genuine interest in learning and in building successful strategies that work for them.
